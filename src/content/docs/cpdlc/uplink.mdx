A message sent from an ATSU to an aircraft is known as an **uplink**. Depending on the [response attribute](#response-attributes) of a given message, an aircraft may or may not have to respond with one of the allowed [downlink](/spec/cpdlc/downlink) messages.


## Response Attributes

<table>
    <thead>
        <tr>
            <th>Resp.</th>
            <th>Description</th>
        </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
        <tr>
            <td style="text-align: right">
                <Badge text="W/U" variant="danger" />
            </td>
            <td>
                **Response Required** with one of the following downlink messages:
                - `DM0 — WILCO`
                - `DM1 — UNABLE`
                - `DM2 — STANDBY`
                - `DM62 — ERROR [error information]`
                - `DM63 — NOT CURRENT DATA AUTHORITY`
                - `DM107 — NOT AUTHORIZED NEXT DATA AUTHORITY`
            </td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td style="text-align: right">
                <Badge text="A/N" variant="danger" />
            </td>
            <td>
                **Response Required** with one of the following downlink messages:
                - `DM2 — STANDBY`
                - `DM4 — AFFIRM`
                - `DM5 — NEGATIVE`
                - `DM62 — ERROR [error information]`
                - `DM63 — NOT CURRENT DATA AUTHORITY`
                - `DM107 — NOT AUTHORIZED NEXT DATA AUTHORITY`
            </td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td style="text-align: right">
                <Badge text="R" variant="danger" />
            </td>
            <td>
                **Response Required** with one of the following downlink messages:
                - `DM1 — UNABLE`
                - `DM2 — STANDBY`
                - `DM3 — ROGER`
                - `DM62 — ERROR [error information]`
                - `DM63 — NOT CURRENT DATA AUTHORITY`
                - `DM107 — NOT AUTHORIZED NEXT DATA AUTHORITY`
            </td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td style="text-align: right">
                <Badge text="Y" variant="danger" />
            </td>
            <td>
                **Response Required** with any downlink message
            </td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td style="text-align: right">
                <Badge text="N" variant="caution" />
            </td>
            <td>
                **Response Not Required**, but one of the following downlink messages could be sent:
                - `DM62 — ERROR [error information]`
                - `DM63 — NOT CURRENT DATA AUTHORITY`
                - `DM107 — NOT AUTHORIZED NEXT DATA AUTHORITY`
            </td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td style="text-align: right">
                <Badge text="NE" variant="success" />
            </td>
            <td>
                **Response Not Required**, and the message should be considered closed even though a response may be downlinked by the aircraft
            </td>
        </tr>
    </tbody>
</table>
