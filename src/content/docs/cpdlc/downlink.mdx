A message sent from an aircraft to an ATSU is known as an **downlink**. Depending on the [response attribute](#response-attributes) of a given message, an ATSU may or may not have to respond with one of the allowed [uplink](/spec/cpdlc/uplink) messages.


## Response Attributes

<table>
    <thead>
        <tr>
            <th>Resp.</th>
            <th>Description</th>
        </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
        <tr>
            <td style="text-align: right">
                <Badge text="Y" variant="danger" />
            </td>
            <td>
                **Response Required** with any uplink message
            </td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td style="text-align: right">
                <Badge text="N" variant="success" />
            </td>
            <td>
                **Response Not Required**, and the message should be considered closed even though a response may be uplinked by the ATSU

                The GOLD specification accomodates logical acknowledgements. Not sure if we want these.
                :::

            </td>
        </tr>
    </tbody>
</table>
